How it all began

It all began 10 years ago during a job transition. At the beginning their was just a large personal vinyl record collection, a very well-loved subuaru and a need for some cash to help pay the bills. From their it evolved to a store front and then to selling to the world-wide-web. All along, the passion to provide the best for the discerning music listener.

Who we are

We are music lovers just like you! We say that it all began with one personal record collection but really it has never changed. We still view our store inventory as our "personal collection" and we view our customers as "our family"!

We care

Inclusivity is a core belief at Fox Records. It’s something we celebrate. We stand for equality and equity, and strive to make our customers and employees feel accepted, respected and welcomed in our store every day. We believe that everyone—every team member, every guest, and every community—deserves to be protected from discrimination, and treated equally. Consistent with these beliefs, we support the federal Equality Act, which provides protections to LGBT individuals, and opposes action that enables discrimination.

Fast delivery

We know that you want your order as soon as possible. With that end in mind, we strive to ship all orders the next business day after receiving your order. Delivery time depends on manner and mode of shipping.

Your security and privacy matters

At Fox Records, we take seriously your security and privacy. We partner with ecommerce services to encrypt all customer data. In the case of a breach of customer data, you will be notified via email with the latest information.
